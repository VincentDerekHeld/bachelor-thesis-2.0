Customers must deactivate the load profile recording in the meter if they refuse an intelligent electricity meter.
Activation or deactivation of load profile recording must be possible remotely.
Each change must be logged in the meter's legal logbook.
If the customer is opt-out, it must be displayed on the meter.
Opt-out requests from customers are implemented at the NL.
Load profile recording is activated or deactivated.
The information is transmitted to the central system as ALARM or EVENT.
A logbook entry is made in the meter.
The OPT-OUT status is displayed on the meter.
