Top management shall review the organization's information security management system.
The management review shall consider the status of actions from previous reviews.
The management review shall consider changes in external and internal issues relevant to the information security management system.
The management review shall consider changes in needs and expectations of interested parties relevant to the information security management system.
The management review shall consider feedback on information security performance.
The management review shall consider trends in nonconformities and corrective actions.
The management review shall consider monitoring and measurement results.
The management review shall consider audit results.
The management review shall consider the fulfilment of information security objectives.
The management review shall consider feedback from interested parties.
The management review shall consider results of risk assessment.
The management review shall consider the status of the risk treatment plan.
The management review shall consider opportunities for continual improvement.
The management review shall decide on continual improvement opportunities.
The management review shall decide on changes to the information security management system.
The organization must document the results of management reviews.
