The organization must determine what needs to be monitored and measured, including information security processes and controls.
The organization must determine the methods for monitoring, measurement, analysis, and evaluation to ensure valid results. These methods should produce comparable and reproducible results.
The organization shall decide when the monitoring and measuring will be performed.
The organization shall decide who will monitor and measure.
The organization shall determine when the results from monitoring and measurement will be analyzed and evaluated.
The organization shall decide who will analyze and evaluate these results.
Documented information must be available as evidence of the results.
The organization must evaluate the information security performance.
The organization must evaluate the effectiveness of the information security management system.
