If the controller becomes aware of a personal data breach, the controller shall notify the supervisory authority within 72 hours, else the controller shall provide reasons for the delay.
Meanwhile, the processor shall notify the controller of a personal data breach without undue delay.
The notification shall describe the nature of the personal data breach.
The notification shall state the categories and approximate number of data subjects concerned and the categories and approximate number of personal data records concerned.
The notification shall communicate the contact details of the data protection officer or other contact point.
The notification shall describe the likely consequences of the personal data breach.
The notification shall describe the measures the controller has taken or proposes to take to address the breach.
If all information cannot be provided at once, the controller may provide it in phases without further delay.
The controller shall document personal data breaches, facts, effects, and remedial actions taken.
