The customer interface (H1) on the meter must be activated or deactivated from the central system.
It is deactivated by default.
The control center receives an information status as ALARM or EVENT.
The customer decides to use or not use the customer interface (H1).
There is an entry in the logbook when the customer interface (H1) on the meter is activated or deactivated.
The meter reports its status to the control panel as ALARM or EVENT.
