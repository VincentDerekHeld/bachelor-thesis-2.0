If the customer decides that the costs are acceptable, the CRS hands out a repair cost calculation back.
If the customer does not decide that the costs are acceptable, she takes her computer home unrepaired.
The first activity is to check and repair the hardware.
The second activity checks and configures the software.
After each activity, the proper system functionality is tested.
If an error is detected, another arbitrary repair activity is executed.
Otherwise, the repair is finished.
