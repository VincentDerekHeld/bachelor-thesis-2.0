If a customer change or open collection claims are given, the central system will remotely interrupt the supply of electrical energy for an object. The terminal will open the breaker present and execute the received command, interrupting the power supply and returning the status to the central system. An entry will also be created in the logbook. If the input is either deregistration of current customer, customer moves out without new tenant, or customer does not pay after admonition process, the supply of electrical energy will be interrupted and the status "interrupted" will be displayed at the terminal and in the central system. Additionally, an entry will be created in the logbook of the terminal.
