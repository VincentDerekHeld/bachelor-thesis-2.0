The controller must obtain consent from the data subject to process their personal data.
Organization will determine methods for monitoring, measuring, analyzing, and evaluating, document results, and decide what needs to be monitored and measured.
ACTOR shall present request for consent distinguishably, understandably, accessibly, and clearly.
The part shall not be binding.
The data subject may withdraw their consent.
Organization shall remain lawful if consent is withdrawn.
The controller must inform the data subject.
The Actor can give and withdraw consent.
The organization must determine if consent to the processing of personal data is freely given when assessing the performance of a contract, including the provision of a service.
