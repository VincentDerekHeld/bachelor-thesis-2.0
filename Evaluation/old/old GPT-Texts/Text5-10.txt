The organization shall determine what needs to be monitored and measured.
The organization shall determine the methods for monitoring, measurement, analysis, and evaluation.
The methods selected should produce comparable and reproducible results.
The organization shall determine when it shall perform the monitoring and measuring.
They shall determine who shall monitor and measure.
The organization shall determine when they shall analyze and evaluate the results from monitoring and measurement.
The organization shall determine who shall analyze and evaluate these results.
Documented information shall serve as evidence of the results.
The organization shall evaluate the information security performance. The organization shall evaluate the effectiveness of the information security management system.
