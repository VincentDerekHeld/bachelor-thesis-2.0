Top management shall review the organization's information security management system at planned intervals.Management shall consider the status of actions. 
Organization must identify changes in external and internal issues relevant to the information security management system. 
Organization must identify changes in needs and expectations of interested parties relevant to the information security management system. 
Organization should receive feedback on the information security performance including trends in nonconformities and corrective actions. 
Organization must monitor and measure results, audit results, and fulfilment of information security objectives. 
Organization should receive feedback from interested parties. 
If risk assessment is conducted, Organization must evaluate results of risk assessment and status of risk treatment plan. 
Organization should identify opportunities for continual improvement. 
Organization must decide on continual improvement opportunities and any needs for changes to the information security management system.
Organization shall monitor, measure, analyze, and evaluate its results if it determines the methods for monitoring, measuring, analyzing, and evaluating the results.
Management shall document the results.
