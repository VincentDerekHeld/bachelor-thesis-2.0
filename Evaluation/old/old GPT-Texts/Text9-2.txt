The data subject shall consent if lawful processing is required.
Processing may be lawful if necessary for a contract with the data subject, to comply with a legal obligation, to protect vital interests, to perform a task in the public interest, to exercise official authority, or for legitimate interests.
Public authorities shall not process when applicable.
The controller shall be subject to the basis for processing.
Organization must determine methods for monitoring, measuring, analyzing, and evaluating, document results, and decide what needs to be monitored and measured.
The organization shall determine the purpose of processing.
The controller must consider legality, types of data, data subjects, entities, purposes, purpose limitation, storage periods, processing operations, and processing procedures.
The Union or Member State shall meet an objective of public interest.
The controller shall consider connection, purposes, context, nature, consequences, and safeguards.
