The controller [must] not prevent the data subject from receiving and transmitting the personal data concerning him or her to another controller when the processing is carried out by automated means.
The organization must determine the methods for monitoring, measuring, analyzing, and evaluating the results.
The data subject has the right to have their personal data transmitted directly from one controller to another, if technically feasible.
The organization must determine the methods for monitoring, measuring, analyzing, and evaluating the results.The organization must carry out the exercise of the right.
The organization must perform a task for the processing to not be applicable to this right.
The controller must mention the right.
