The party sends a warrant possession request.
 The request and retrieves the SCT file.
 The SCT Warrant Possession is forwarded to Queensland Police.
 The SCT physical file is stored by the Back Office awaiting a report to be sent by the Police.
 The report is received.
 Then, Back Office attaches the new SCT document, and stores the expanded SCT physical file.
 After that, some other MC internal staff receives the physical SCT file.
