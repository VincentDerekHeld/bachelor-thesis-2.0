Players want to create custom farming bots.
 First you have to find out which resources you want.
 Up to 10 resources in parallel can be collected, some resources are more urgently needed than others.
 Some resources are required to create tools.
 create a custom logic of the dependencies in your game
 After all preferences are collected, your bot starts farming.
 Sometimes, random natural disasters strike and set your bot back.
 You are notified.
 During farming it is possible to update the list of to be farmed materials. After the bot is finished, you should be able to brag to selected friends and/or send them materials.
